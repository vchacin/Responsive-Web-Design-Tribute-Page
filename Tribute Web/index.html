The Divine Comedy
“All hope abandon, ye who enter here.” ― Dante Alighieri
Map of Hell, Sandro Botticelli
Structure
  • Inferno: concentric circles of Hell
    1. Limbo
    2. Lust
    3. Gluttony
    4. Greed
    5. Wrath
    6. Heresy
    7. Violence
      1. Ring 1: Against Neighbors
      2. Ring 2: Against Self
      3. Ring 3: Against God, Art, and Nature
    8. Fraud
      1. Bolgia 1 – Panderers and seducers
      2. Bolgia 2 – Flatterers
      3. Bolgia 3 – Simoniacs
      4. Bolgia 4 – Sorcerers
      5. Bolgia 5 – Barrators
      6. Bolgia 6 – Hypocrites
      7. Bolgia 7 – Thieves
      8. Bolgia 8 – Counsellors of Fraud
      9. Bolgia 9 – Sowers of Discord
      10. Bolgia 10 – Falsifiers
    9. Treachery
      1. Round 1 – Caina: Traitors to their Kindred
      2. Round 2 – Antenora: Traitors to their Country
      3. Round 3 – Ptolomaea: Traitors to their Guests
      4. Round 4 – Judecca: Traitors to their Lords
      5. Round 3 – Ptolomaea: Traitors to their Lords
  • Ante-Purgatory
    1. Shore of the Island
    2. The Excommunicate
    3. The Late-Repentant
  • Purgatory: terraces of Mount Purgatory
    1. First terrace (Pride)
    2. Second terrace (Envy)
    3. Third terrace (Wrath)
    4. Fourth terrace (Sloth)
    5. Fifth terrace (Avarice)
    6. Sixth terrace (Gluttony)
    7. Seventh terrace (Lust)
  • Paradiso: celestial spheres of Heaven
    1. The Moon: The Inconstant
    2. Mercury: The Ambitious
    3. Venus: The Lovers
    4. The Sun: The Wise
    5. Mars: The Warriors of the Faith
    6. Jupiter: The Just Rulers
    7. Saturn: The Contemplatives
    8. The Fixed Stars: Faith, Hope, and Love
    9. The Primum Mobile: The Angels
